02

Key Improvements Over Claude 1.0

📖
100,000 Token Context
Claude 2.0 can process roughly 75,000 words in a single prompt — entire books, codebases, or hundreds of pages of documentation. Claude 1.0 was limited to ~9,000 tokens, making long-document analysis impossible in a single pass.
11× larger context
🛡️
Constitutional AI Safety
2× better at producing harmless responses vs Claude 1.3. Trained with Anthropic's Constitutional AI framework and RLHF refinements, Claude 2.0 declines harmful requests more reliably without becoming overly restrictive.
2× safer
💻
Advanced Code Generation
From 56% to 71.2% on HumanEval — a massive leap for developers. Claude 2.0 writes multi-file programs, debugs complex logic, understands modern frameworks, and generates production-quality code across Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, Rust, and more.
+27% coding improvement
✍️
Long-Form Document Creation
Claude 2.0 generates coherent long documents — memos, legal briefs, technical reports, and stories — all in a single generation. Earlier models struggled to maintain coherence beyond a few paragraphs without losing track of structure or context.
Sustained coherence
🎓
Graduate-Level Reasoning
Scoring above the 90th percentile on GRE Reading and Writing, with solid performance on quantitative reasoning. Claude 2.0's training on richer, more recent data allows it to reason across domains with nuance that v1.0 couldn't achieve.
Top 10% on GRE
🌐
More Current Knowledge
Trained on data extending into early 2023, Claude 2.0 knows about modern libraries, recent events, and newer programming frameworks. Claude 1.0's knowledge cutoff left significant gaps for developers working with current tools.
Updated training data
